## What it does / when to pick it
Auto-sniffs and applies coupon codes at checkout across thousands of stores, plus optional cashback, plus an "identical product cheaper from another Amazon seller" nudge. Formerly named **"Coupons at Checkout."** Pick only as a third/long-tail layer behind Capital One Shopping + Karma, and only after the **ownership caveat** below.

## General experience & gotchas (踩坑)
- **Owned by System1 ad-tech** (via NextGen Shopping LLC, copyright "© 2026 NextGen Shopping LLC, A System1 Company" on the official site). System1 is a search/ad monetization company → **data-collection concern is the headline caveat.** Requires all-sites access (can read/modify any page). Flag this before recommending; many privacy-aware users will prefer Capital One Shopping or Karma instead.
- **Last-click affiliate mechanic, FLAG.** Same checkout affiliate-link / cashback-attribution rewrite as the rest of the domain; can overwrite a creator referral or compete with a cashback portal. Don't double-count extension savings + public-coupon savings.
- **Coupon-apply quality is average**, fine as a long-tail filler, not a leader on success rate.
- **Two-extension conflict**, do not run alongside Capital One Shopping / Karma / Honey / RetailMeNot; same checkout-slot fight, last-loaded wins.

## Failure signals & fallback
If a code isn't applied or "savings" don't land at checkout, verify manually. **Fallback:** Capital One Shopping / Karma (preferred, cleaner data posture) or Coupert.
